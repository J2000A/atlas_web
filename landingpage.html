Customer Need

The decision process for new mobility projects is very complex and currently there exists no suitable support tool which helps politics & municipalities and practitioners for an overview about mobility injustice.

Our solution addresses all bottlenecks and at the same time: JustFairMobility is understandable, transparent and user-friendly and enables citizens to participate at the mobility planning process.

Value Proposition

JFM seamlessly bridges web and app interfaces, uncovering comprehensive and user-friendly data to eliminate bottlenecks and enable informed, sustainable, and long-term oriented decisions.

  1. Quantify mobility injustice data
    • Data put into context
    • Added Points of Interest (e.g., bus stops)
  2. Explore critical areas by leveraging qualitative data
    • Explore critical areas by leveraging qualitative data
    • JFM app fosters citizen participation
    • Municipalities get direct feedback from citizens
  3. Make injustice visible
    • Combination of qualitative and quantitative data
    • Great UI and UX in web and mobile app
  4. Take conclusive action
    • Identify real pain points as decision support

Prototype

Our prototype is composed out of two parts. The JustFairMobility mobile Android app that allow citizens to report problems and the JustFairMobility Atlas web app for politicians and municipalities. The Firebase backend connects the mobile app with the web app.

Differentiation

Currently, there is no solution that provides aggregated and interpretable mobility data at scale. If a critical number of users is reached, monetization and a freemium concept for use of JFM are planned. This enables the transfer of politicians, municipalities, and practitioners from users to clients. An urgent go-to-market strategy and investments in scalable software solutions are imperative to sustainable growth and venture success.
